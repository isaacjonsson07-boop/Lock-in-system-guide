Section 1

How the App Works

The Structured Achievement app is where you run your system. The 21-day program installs your operating system — direction, habits, non-negotiables, priorities, identity. The app is where you execute it, every day, after installation is complete.

The app has five tabs. Each one serves a specific function in your operating system. Nothing extra, nothing decorative. Every screen exists to keep you operational.

Today
Your daily execution surface. Non-negotiables, habits, tasks, journal. Open it, execute, close it.
Installation
The 21-day lesson corridor. One day at a time, with a lesson tab and a journal tab for each.
Reviews
Weekly snapshot of your data. Quarterly recalibration to rotate habits, update priorities, and realign your direction.
Achievements
Monthly System Reports. Your score, tier, and operational history — measured and recorded.
System
Your living documents. Direction statement, identity, priorities, decision framework, operating manual. Plus your non-negotiables and habits.

Section 2

Today — Your Daily Execution Surface

This is the tab you open every morning and every evening. It shows today's date, your direction statement at the top, and three sections: non-negotiables, keystone habits, and daily tasks.

Non-Negotiables
Your core daily commitments — the things you do regardless of how you feel. These show up every day. Tap to check them off. If you see a zero, the day is incomplete.
Keystone Habits
Recurring behaviors tied to specific days of the week. They only appear on the days you configured them for. Tap to complete.
Daily Tasks
One-off tasks for today. Add them the night before or in the morning. Tap to complete, swipe or delete when done. These are your tactical execution items — what specifically needs to happen today.

The Today tab — direction, non-negotiables, and habits.

How to use it

Morning: open the Today tab. See your non-negotiables, habits, and tasks. Execute them throughout the day, checking each off as you go. Evening: add tomorrow's tasks. Close the app. That's the entire daily interaction — under two minutes.

Section 3

Installation — The 21-Day Corridor

The Installation tab contains all 21 days of the program. Each day appears as a numbered card in a grid, organized by phase: Stabilize (Days 1–7), Reconstruct (Days 8–14), and Install (Days 15–21). Days 7, 14, and 21 are review days marked with a dashed border.

Click any day to open it. Each day has two tabs:

Lesson
The day's content — a principle, deep explanation, and configuration tasks. Read it fully. The configuration tasks at the end are not optional — they are what installs the system component.
Journal
Structured prompts specific to that day. Your answers here are how you internalize and apply the lesson to your life. Complete the journal before moving to the next day.

The 21-day grid — three phases, progress tracking, and stats.

Day 1's lesson — principle, content, and configuration tasks.

Pacing

One day per day. Do not skip ahead. Each lesson builds on the previous one. Rushing through three days in one sitting installs nothing — it just creates the illusion of progress. The system needs time to integrate. Respect the sequence.

Section 4

Reviews — Weekly and Quarterly

The Reviews tab has three sub-tabs: Snapshot, Weekly, and Quarterly. This is where you assess how your system is running and make adjustments.

Snapshot
A live dashboard showing the last 7 days. System health percentage, daily completion bars, non-negotiable consistency per item, and category breakdown (NNs, habits, tasks). Glance at this anytime to see where you stand.
Weekly Review
Four structured questions: what worked, where you drifted, one adjustment for next week, and your single focus. Takes 10 minutes. Do this every Sunday. Your weekly data shows alongside the questions so you review with evidence, not guesswork.
Quarterly Recalibration
A 5-step guided flow that walks through your direction, non-negotiables, habits, priorities, and identity. You can keep or rotate each non-negotiable and habit directly — changes apply immediately. Add new ones using the same form as the System tab. Do this every 3 months.

The Snapshot — 7-day system health and category rates.

Habit Audit — keep what works, rotate what's stale.

Section 5

Achievements — Monthly System Reports

At the end of each month, you generate a System Report — a scored summary of how your system performed. The report calculates a weighted score based on three categories: habit completion (40%), task completion (35%), and non-negotiable completion (25%).

Your score earns a tier:

Diamond — 100
Perfect execution. Every habit, every task, every non-negotiable. No misses.
Gold — 75 to 99
Strong operational month. Consistent execution with minor gaps.
Silver — 50 to 74
System ran but needs tightening. Review where consistency dropped.
Bronze — below 50
Significant drift. Revisit your non-negotiables and daily structure.

Monthly report history — scores, tiers, and progress.

Expanded report — breakdown, stats, and personal highlight.

Minimums

To earn above Gold, your system must meet minimum thresholds: at least 3 active habits, an average of 3 tasks per day, and at least 2 non-negotiables. If you're below these minimums, your score caps at 75 regardless of completion rates. This prevents gaming the score with a single habit and one task.

If you forget to generate a report before the month ends, the app automatically creates it for you when you next open the Achievements tab. No data is lost.

Section 6

System Patches — Post-Installation Content

After you complete the 21-day installation, your system is operational — but it's not static. System Patches are weekly lessons that unlock one week after you finish Day 21. They address the challenges that only surface once the system is running: maintaining momentum, handling disruptions, recalibrating after setbacks, and deepening the infrastructure you built.

4 weekly patches
One new patch unlocks each week for four weeks after installation. They appear in the Installation tab alongside your 21 days. Each patch is a focused lesson with specific configuration tasks — the same format as the installation, applied to operational-phase challenges.
Time-gated delivery
Patches unlock automatically based on when you completed Day 21. You don't need to do anything — they appear when they're ready. This pacing ensures you've had real operational experience before each patch addresses the next challenge.

Section 7

System — Your Operating Documents

The System tab is your operating system's configuration panel. It has three sections, all of which can be open simultaneously.

System Documents
Six living documents built during installation: Direction Statement, Identity Statement, Priority Stack, Decision Framework, Failure Protocol, and Operating Manual. Each one corresponds to a specific lesson day. Click to view, click the pencil to edit. Update these during quarterly recalibrations as your system evolves.
Non-Negotiables
Your daily commitments. Add new ones with the "Add non-negotiable" button — type the title and hit Add. Delete by hovering over any item and clicking the trash icon. These appear on the Today tab every day.
Keystone Habits
Recurring habits with day-of-week and time configuration. Click "Add keystone habit" to create one — set the name, time, and which days it should appear. These show up on the Today tab only on their scheduled days.

The System tab — your documents, non-negotiables, and habits in one configuration panel.

Section 8

The Daily Rhythm

This is the operational loop that makes the system work. Two touchpoints per day, under two minutes each. Everything else runs in the background.

Morning — 60 seconds
Open the Today tab.
See your non-negotiables, habits scheduled for today, and any tasks you added last night. This is your operating brief. You now know exactly what today requires. Start executing.
Throughout the day
Check items off as you complete them.
Tap each non-negotiable, habit, and task as you finish it. This takes seconds. The completion data feeds your weekly snapshot, monthly reports, and quarterly recalibrations.
Evening — 60 seconds
Plan tomorrow.
Add tomorrow's tasks. Review if anything was left incomplete today — if it was, decide whether it rolls to tomorrow or gets dropped. Close the app. You're done.
Sunday — 10 minutes
Weekly review.
Open the Reviews tab. Check your snapshot data. Complete the four weekly review questions. Save. This is your system maintenance cycle — a structured check-in that prevents drift.
Every 3 months — 25 minutes
Quarterly recalibration.
Open the Quarterly tab in Reviews. Walk through all five steps: direction, non-negotiables, habits, priorities, identity. Rotate what's stale. Add what's missing. Realign with where you are now, not where you were three months ago.
